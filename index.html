01 — How I got here

I loved the growth side — funnels, experiments, finding what moves the needle. But I kept wanting to be closer to the thing itself.

Product sits exactly between growth and engineering, so I went all in. When Claude and Cursor made building real, I gave myself six months to learn the entire pipeline — ideation, design, Swift, backend, App Store, and the zero-budget marketing that gets an app its first users.

It started with an ambitious VC-style bet that taught me when to pivot. Then I shipped two more across completely different niches, to learn how to bootstrap, move fast, and grow with no money. I owned every part of all three.

APP 01
Shopping · AI vision · The big bet

Haul AI

See it. Scan it. Discover what you love.

Haul started as my ambitious one: a social platform for fashion creators, Pinterest meets TikTok. The traction didn't come — so I pivoted to a utility. Now you share any outfit from anywhere and AI instantly identifies every piece, faster and sharper than Google Lens for fashion.

The real lesson wasn't the model. It was learning to read the market and kill my favourite idea when it was time.

APP 03
Productivity · Developer tool · Live

CodeFocus

Your feeds stay locked until your AI is working.

The anti-distraction app for developers who'd rather their AI did the work. TikTok, Instagram, X — whatever eats your day — stay blocked. They only unlock while an AI coding agent like Claude Code, Codex or Cursor is actively running on your machine.

Built around a Mac-to-iPhone Bluetooth link and Apple's Screen Time API. My current bet — and the most technical thing I've shipped.
